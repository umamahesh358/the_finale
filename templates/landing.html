About CIET

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Chalapathi Institute of Engineering & Technology, established in 1995, is one of the premier engineering institutions in Andhra Pradesh. Located in Guntur, CIET is affiliated to JNTUK, Kakinada, and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

With NAAC accreditation and NBA-accredited programs, the institute offers a strong academic foundation, world-class infrastructure, and industry-oriented training to shape future-ready engineers and professionals.
